Village Pethia, Tanda, District Ambedkar Nagar – 224190 (Uttar Pradesh) is a rural location in the Ambedkar Nagar district of Uttar Pradesh, India. The village lies near the town of Tanda, which is known for its cultural heritage and textile activities. Ambedkar Nagar district is situated in the eastern part of Uttar Pradesh and is named after Dr. B. R. Ambedkar. The area mainly consists of agricultural land, and farming is an important occupation for many residents. People in the village live in a close-knit community and follow traditional customs and festivals. Basic facilities like schools, small markets, and health centers are available in nearby towns. Tanda town serves as a local hub for trade, education, and transportation for nearby villages including Pethia. The region is connected by local roads and public transport to other parts of the district. Villagers depend on agriculture, small businesses, and local industries for their livelihood. Overall, Village Pethia is a peaceful rural area that reflects the traditional lifestyle and culture of Uttar Pradesh.
