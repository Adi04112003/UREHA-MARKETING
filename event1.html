Nukkad Program

A Nukkad Program (Nukkad Natak) is a type of street play performed in public places like streets, markets, schools, or college campuses. It is usually performed by a group of people to spread awareness about social issues. These plays are simple, short, and easy to understand so that common people can watch them easily. Nukkad programs often focus on topics like cleanliness, education, women empowerment, child labour, and environmental protection. The actors perform without a stage and interact directly with the audience. This makes the performance more engaging and impactful. Such programs are widely used in India to educate people and encourage positive social change. They also help students develop confidence, communication skills, and teamwork. Many schools, colleges, and organizations organize Nukkad programs during awareness campaigns and cultural events. Overall, a Nukkad Program is an effective and creative way to deliver important messages to society.
