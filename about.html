About Us

Know more about Ureha Marketing Pvt. Ltd.

UREHA MARKETING PVT. LTD.

Ureha Marketing Pvt. Ltd. (UMPL) is a registered private limited company based in Uttar Pradesh, India. With our registered office at 121-Lakhanpur Housing Society, Vikas Nagar, Kanpur and our operational headquarters at Village Pethia, Tanda, Distt. Ambedkar Nagar, we serve a wide range of clients across the state.

The company is GST registered, reflecting our commitment to compliance and transparency in all business dealings.

Tanda has a rich history of trade and commerce and is well-connected to key markets across Uttar Pradesh. Ureha Marketing plays an important role in the supply and distribution network of this region, serving local businesses and traders efficiently.

Ureha Marketing supplies its services primarily to clients in the local market and adjacent districts of Uttar Pradesh. In addition, the company also serves clients in neighboring states, maintaining strong business relationships built on trust and reliability.

The company is committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service. Our team is dedicated to ensuring complete client satisfaction at every step of the process.
